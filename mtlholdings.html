OUR
COMPANY

MTL Holdings Inc. was established in January 2018 by a group of entrepreneurs that is committed to provide solutions for companies’ Accounting & Human Resource needs.

At MTL Holdings Inc., we are driven and inspired to serve with full enthusiasm as we strive to develop a mutually rewarding relationship with our employees, partners, suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates & customers. Our competent, dependable and experienced team provides Finance & Human Resource needs through thorough and robust working approach, proactive communication & commitment to quality.

OUR TEAM

Our people are our greatest asset as we believe that adding value to our clients requires quality people. Our highly-skilled and talented staff enhance our ability to render incomparable services to our clients.
