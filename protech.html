OUR MISSION

At Protech Coating Specialist, Inc., we define our mission as an everyday challenging part of the business. It is our mission to provide our customers the highest level of quality surface finish at a fair and competitive price. We are equally dedicated to ensuring exceptional customer service by keeping our employees motivated, inspired, focused and have the “WE” attitude who understands the importance of individual and customized service. We always aim to understand, respond and better serve all customers' needs.

We aim to offer nothing but the highest level of professionalism, integrity, honesty, and fairness. We serve with passion and dedication as we cultivate a mutual strong relationship with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates and customers.

At Protech Coating Specialist, Inc., we are dedicated to living and working by this principle. It is not just providing the excellent standard of work, but also valuing the individuals who are the “backbone” for every success that the company may achieve. It is our mission to strengthen employees' character building and continuously nourish their moral values that will serve as the organization's unified identity. We understand that we are only as good as the products and services that we provide to our customers and we strive to provide only the best in the industry.

ABOUTOUR COMPANY

Protech Coating Specialist, Inc. started operations on February 2011 after a group of enterprising people in the coating industry finds the need to improve the powder coating system to meet the high quality coating demands of the growing architectural, appliance, automotive, electrical, furniture and metal fabrication industry. The company is involved in quality powder coatings of steel and aluminum component parts and fixtures. Protech Coating Specialist, Inc. is a powder coating contractor which employs pretreatment, powder coating and curing of parts used in the architectural, appliance, automotive, electrical, furniture and metal fabrication market.
