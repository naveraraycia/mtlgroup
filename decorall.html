Who we are

Decorall Coating Corporation is a coating contractor which employs pretreatment, powder coating and curing of parts used in the architectural, appliance, automotive and furniture market.

Decorall Coating Corporation started after a group of enterprising people in the coating industry finds the need to improve quality and speed of servicing the coating requirements of the growing architectural, appliance, automotive, furniture and metal fabrication industry.

Our Services

We specialize in providing Powder Coating Services for the following industries:

  • Architecture 01
  • Appliances 02
  • Furniture & Fixtures 03
  • Automotive 04
  • Racking 05

The architectural and building market powder coats products such as aluminum extrusion used on frames for windows and doors and modular furniture.

The appliance industry benefits from powder coating on front and side panels of ranges and refrigerators, washer tops and lids, dryer drums, air conditioner cabinets, water heaters, dishwasher racks, and cavities of microwave ovens.

Most of the Furniture & Fixture preferred to be powder coated. Some of these items include table, chair, desk steel bed, cpu case, cabinet etc.

The automotive industry uses powder coating on wheels, bumpers, hubcaps, door handles, decorative trim and accent parts, truck beds, filters, and numerous engine parts.

The Racking Industry powder coat their upright frames, load beams, diagonal/horizontal braces, pallet support, wire decking, row spacer, column protector etc.
